Welcome to RDA Bulk Packaging India Private Limited.

RDA Bulk Packaging India Pvt. Ltd. offers a wide range of FIBCs (flexible intermediate bulk containers), also known as bulk bags. The company is a sister company of RDA Bulk Packaging Ltd., which has the idea of supplying high-quality bulk packaging material and has successfully been catering to customer's needs for the last 25 years. We now bring this wealth of experience and expertise worldwide, delivering premium quality and service directly to large-volume buyers across the globe. We are experts in optimising companies' filling, transport, and storage conditions, offering economically profitable and environmentally friendly solutions. In addition to FIBCs, we offer a variety of other products, including woven polypropylene sacks, sandbags, garden waste collection bags, polythene bags, liners, and more. We can also manufacture products to meet specific customer requirements on a contractual basis. They are well-equipped to cater to all industries with their diverse range of bags, ensuring that all needs are met.

RDA 100% rPP

At RDA Bulk Packaging India Pvt. Ltd., we are proud to introduce a groundbreaking line of products: 100% rPP bags. These bags are a testament to our dedication to sustainability and reducing the environmental impact of packaging materials. 100% rPP stands for 100% recycled polypropylene, a material derived from post-consumer and post-industrial waste. By harnessing the power of recycling, we have crafted a range of bags that serve their purpose effectively and contribute significantly to a circular economy.
Our Range of Products: Discover the unparalleled strength and versatility of our 100% rPP bags, thoughtfully designed to cater to diverse packaging needs. With these bags, you can be confident that your packaging needs will be met, no matter how diverse or demanding they may be.
